Dance Classes For
Beginners

If you love to dance and are not exactly sure where to start,you are in the right place! Our dance classes for beginners are designed especially for you. The classes are a mix of a warm up,technical knowledge and dance choreography to upbeat music. Through these fun dance classes for beginners you will be introduced to the foundation and basics of hiphop technique, which is a great first step for people new to dance.


Intermediate Dance Classes

For dancers 2-3 years experience,The class emphasizes technique, artistry, and athleticism.Focus on breaking down turns, leaps, placement, connecting movement so it flows, and building a dance vocabulary that will bring the dance student to the next level.

Advanced Dance Classes

For dancers wanting to perfect the basics of dance and move to higher technical and musical complexity. The class has divided into warm-up,flooring, dance techniques and choreography.
