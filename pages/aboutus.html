ABOUT US

The Freshdance Academy was set up by Indian-Australian choreographer Ashley Lobo
in November of 1998 to teach dance along the lines of dance Institutes overseas. Today,
The Freshdance Academy has a huge student database spanning New Delhi,NCR and Mumbai.

THE PHILOSOPHY

The philosophy of the Academy is "Helping people find and build their dance & themselves,
to full potential,in an environment of fun, love & values".

Students are guided to apply the self-belief,discipline and
goal settings kills that they have discovered through dance into their chosen vacations,
be it dance or any other.

THE VISION

"Yo create a higher awareness & understanding of international
dance and to facilitiate & help in developing constantly evolving artists".

The Academy believes in the facilitiation of evolving artists.Students
are taught that dance is not limited to steps alone,but includes a wider understanding
of self, creativity, movement and physiology.This information is to enable them to
create their own work and eventually discover and develop a movement style of their own.
